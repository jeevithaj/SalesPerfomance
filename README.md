# Plant Co Sales Performance Report (Power BI)

## 🌱 Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Power BI report analyzing Plant Co’s sales performance from 2022 to 2024. The dashboard focuses on year‑to‑date (YTD) trends, profitability insights, and customer behavior across countries, products, and account types. It is designed to help business stakeholders quickly identify performance gaps and opportunities for improvement.

---

## 🎯 Key Features & Visuals

### 1. **Gross Profit YTD vs Previous YTD — Waterfall Chart**
- Highlights year‑over‑year changes in Gross Profit.
- Breaks down positive and negative contributors to overall performance.

### 2. **Bottom 10 Countries by YTD vs Previous YTD — Treemap**
- Identifies underperforming markets.
- Helps prioritize regions requiring strategic attention.

### 3. **Gross Profit YTD vs Previous by Month & Product — Stacked Column Chart**
- Shows monthly performance trends.
- Compares product categories to reveal seasonal or product‑level shifts.

### 4. **Gross Profit YTD & Gross Profit % by Account Type — Scatter Plot**
- Visualizes profitability distribution across account types.
- Helps distinguish high‑value vs low‑margin customer groups.

---

## 🔄 Dynamic Metric Switching
All major visuals include a toggle that allows users to switch between:
- **Sales**
- **Quantity**
- **Gross Profit**

This interactivity enables flexible analysis depending on the business question.
